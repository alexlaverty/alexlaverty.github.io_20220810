Narrative

A little information about Nora Laverty. She was born to John Goldspring and Elizabeth Donnelly nee Robertson or Robinson in about 1880. No birth certificate has been found for Nora or her younger brothers and sisters. however there is a record of the death of Patrick Goldspring aged 17 months from whooping cough at Swanvale near Inverell in 1879. Nora was married to Denis Laverty on 10th April 1901 at Taylors Arm.The groom was 42 years old and the bride was 20.The bride's father, John Goldspring gave his consent to the marriage. The certificate states that both bride and groom were born at Macksville, and that the bride's name was Honora, however on Nora's death certificate (1944) her brithplace is said to be Emmaville. Cause of her death was cerebral haemorrhage.
source : Margaret Sharpe - Descendants of Nora and Denis Laverty, Facebook Group
