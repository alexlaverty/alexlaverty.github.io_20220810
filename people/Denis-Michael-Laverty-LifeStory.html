Summary

When Denis Michael Laverty was born on 19 March 1859 in Cleator Moor, Cumberland, his father, Michael, was 33 and his mother, Sarah, was 28. He married Honora (Norah) Goldspring Laverty on 10 April 1901 in Bowraville, New South Wales. They had 12 children in 15 years. He died on 16 August 1920 in Taylors Arm, New South Wales, at the age of 61.
