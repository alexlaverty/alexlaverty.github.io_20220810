Summary

Johann Georg Koch was born on 14 April 1792 in Birkenau, Hesse, Germany, the son of Katharina and Johann. He had one son and one daughter with Elisabetha Störmer. He died on 22 March 1859 in Germany at the age of 66, and was buried in Schallbach, Baden-Württemberg, Germany.
