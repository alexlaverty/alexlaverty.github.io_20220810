Summary

When Johann Peter Koch was born on 27 May 1825 in Mannheim, Baden-Wrttemberg, Germany, his father, Johann, was 33 and his mother, Elisabetha, was 23. He married Anna Katharina Schroder on 12 August 1852 in Wallstadt, Baden-Wrttemberg, Germany. They had 12 children in 23 years. He died on 17 November 1909 in Kempsey, New South Wales, having lived a long life of 84 years, and was buried in Stuarts Point, New South Wales.
